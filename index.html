Who We Are

Infrastructure Strength with Digital Vision

Led by Özcan Cakir, Cakir IT-Solutions operates from Zele, Belgium and brings together infrastructure execution and digital transformation services under one roof. Since 2018, we have been active in directional drilling, technical installations, field coordination and operational support.

Our expertise goes beyond field work. Through our IT division, we develop corporate websites, internal tools, tracking systems and software solutions tailored to operational businesses. With a Belgium-based structure, we are connected to projects and collaborations in the Netherlands and Germany, while continuing to grow our customer network in Turkey.
