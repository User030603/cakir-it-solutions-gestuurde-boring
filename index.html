Who We Are

A Software/Mechanic Engineering Company

Founded by Özcan Çakır, Cakir IT-Solutions is not your typical software agency. We have deep roots in the infrastructure sector, executing complex Horizontaal Gestuurde Boring (Directional Drilling) projects since 2018.

In November 2025, we officially launched our IT Division to solve the problems we faced in the field. Today, we build custom Kotlin Android Apps and Web Platforms to digitize logistics and construction workflows.
